## What Is Home Sample Collection?

Home sample collection is exactly what it sounds like: instead of visiting a lab, a certified phlebotomist (a trained blood-draw specialist) visits your home or office to collect your blood, urine, or other samples. The samples are then transported to our NABL-accredited laboratory in sealed, temperature-controlled containers and processed exactly as they would be in-centre.

Your report is then emailed and WhatsApp'd to you — usually within 4–6 hours for most blood tests.

Home collection is especially useful for:

## What Happens to Your Sample After Collection?

Once collected, your sample is:

1. **Labelled and barcoded** at your home to ensure zero mix-ups
2. **Transported in temperature-controlled carriers** to our central processing lab
3. **Processed by our NABL-accredited laboratory team**
4. **Reviewed by a senior pathologist** before the report is issued
5. **Sent to you digitally** via email and WhatsApp

The entire chain — from collection to delivery — is tracked and logged for quality assurance.
