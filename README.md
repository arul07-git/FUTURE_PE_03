## Project Overview

Most diagnostic centre websites fail to attract local patients online because:

- They rely only on word-of-mouth or referrals
- They post random blog content without SEO intent
- They don't target local search terms like "blood test centre near me in Chennai"
- They have no content cluster — just isolated articles

This SEO Content Pack solves all of that by delivering:

| Deliverable | Description |
|---|---|
| 1 Pillar Blog | Long-form authority article targeting the primary keyword |
| 4 Supporting Blogs | Cluster articles targeting specific services and FAQs |
| Keyword + Intent Map | All target keywords with search intent classification |
| Internal Linking Plan | How every blog connects to strengthen domain authority |
| Reusable Prompt Templates | Ready-to-use prompts for any future diagnostic client |

## Content Cluster Structure

```
PILLAR BLOG (Main)
├── Best Diagnostic Centre in Chennai — Services, Tests & What to Expect
│
├── SUPPORTING BLOG 1
│   └── Full Body Health Checkup Packages in Chennai — What's Included & Cost
│
├── SUPPORTING BLOG 2
│   └── Blood Test at Home in Chennai — How It Works & What to Expect
│
├── SUPPORTING BLOG 3
│   └── Thyroid Test Cost in Chennai — TSH, T3, T4 & When to Get Tested
│
└── SUPPORTING BLOG 4
    └── How to Prepare for a Blood Test — Fasting Rules, Dos & Don'ts
```

Every supporting blog links back to the pillar blog.
The pillar blog links out to all four supporting blogs.
This creates a hub-and-spoke model that Google rewards with higher rankings.

## Internal Linking Strategy

```
Pillar Blog
  → Links TO: Supporting Blog 1, 2, 3, 4

Supporting Blog 1 → Links BACK TO: Pillar Blog
                  → Cross-links TO: Supporting Blog 2

Supporting Blog 2 → Links BACK TO: Pillar Blog
                  → Cross-links TO: Supporting Blog 4

Supporting Blog 3 → Links BACK TO: Pillar Blog
                  → Cross-links TO: Supporting Blog 4

Supporting Blog 4 → Links BACK TO: Pillar Blog
                  → Cross-links TO: Supporting Blog 2, 3
```

**Rule:** Every blog must contain at least 2 internal links. The pillar blog should contain 4–6.

## Local SEO Strategy

To rank for searches in Chennai, every blog:

1. **Mentions Chennai naturally** in H1, first paragraph, and at least 2 body sections
2. **Uses location-service combos** like "blood test at home in Chennai" and "diagnostic centre in Anna Nagar / Velachery / T. Nagar"
3. **Targets neighbourhood-level searches** — major Chennai neighbourhoods are mentioned where relevant
4. **Answers local intent questions** — "open on Sunday", "NABL accredited", "home collection"
5. **Includes a local CTA** — "Book your test at our Chennai centre today"

## How to Reuse for Other Clients

To adapt this entire content pack for a different city or business:

1. Replace `Chennai` with the target city throughout all files
2. Replace `LifeCare Diagnostics` with the actual business name
3. Update the neighbourhood names in local SEO sections
4. Replace keyword costs/prices with locally accurate figures
5. Run the prompts in `prompts/prompt-templates.md` with the new variables filled in

This system works for: **diagnostic centres, dental clinics, physiotherapy centres, eye care clinics, hospitals, and any local health service business.**
